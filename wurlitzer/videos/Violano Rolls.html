Violano, Early Roll List

Introduction

This list contains a list of the contents of many of the early Violano Rolls. It is necessarily incomplete because the original data is not available. It was published in monthly bulletins between 1909 and 1925 when the Violano was in production.
Roll numbers outside this range are relatively recent.
