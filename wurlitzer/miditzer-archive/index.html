Allen Theatre Organ Archive (November 2024)

This is an archive of Allen organ Midi files. They were donated by Craig Williams from his extensive collection.
These midi files contain all of their original controls.

All the music can be played by eplayOrgan's TH317+V1-1 organ. or a suitable Allen organ. Download and import this organ into your eplayOrgan64.

You can also use eplayOrgan64 to Translate these files to play correctly on other organs.

Translated Allen Files

The Allen Files below have been translated to play on ePlayOrgan's Universal organ. Book Music Archive (January 2025)

Book Music was produced in books with folding cardboard pages. It originally played on Large European Dance Organs (Often known as "Dutch Organs") made around 100 years ago by companies such as Mortier.

The Book Music Files below have been translated to play on ePlayOrgan's Universal organ. This means that they will play on the Universal organ or any of eplayOrgan's other similar organs - e.g. Miditzer260 - by telling the playing organ to translate the input from Universal to Current. Use Options>Setup MIDI I/O>Midi Input>Translate

Book Music Translated Book Music files.

Original controls are included but these files currently do not play the percussion or Accordion channels.
