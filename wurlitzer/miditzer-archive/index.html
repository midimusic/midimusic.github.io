Book Music Archive (February 2025)

Book Music was produced in books with folding cardboard pages. It originally played on Large European Dance Organs (Often known as "Dutch Organs") made around 100 years ago by companies such as Mortier.

All the original Book Music can be played using Book Music organ package v1-0. Download this package and follow installation instructions in the "installing organs" file. (I recommend turning the organ gains down to 0.2 to avoid clipping - to be corrected in the next version).
